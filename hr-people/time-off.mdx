We have an unlimited PTO policy for salaried employees. Unlimited means we trust you to take what you need—it doesn't mean time off is unlimited in practice. Most employees take three to five weeks per year, plus holidays.

## Types of leave

**Vacation / personal time** — No fixed accrual. Take time off by following the request process below.

**Sick leave** — Use sick days when you're ill or need to care for a sick family member. No approval needed. Just notify your manager and mark it in the HR portal.

**Company holidays** — We observe approximately 10 federal holidays per year. The full calendar is posted in the HR portal each January.

**Parental leave** — See the [benefits page](/hr-people/benefits) for paid leave details.

**Bereavement** — Up to 5 days paid with no approval needed. Contact HR for longer situations.

**Jury duty / civic leave** — Fully paid. Send documentation to HR.

## Requesting time off

1. Check with your manager first for any time-sensitive periods (launches, on-call rotations, etc.).
2. Submit your request in the HR portal at least **two weeks in advance** for absences longer than two days.
3. Block your calendar and set an out-of-office reply.
4. For sick days, submit in the portal on the day or the next business day.

## Blackout periods

Some teams have blackout periods around major releases or customer commitments. Your manager will communicate these in advance. Requests during a blackout period require manager and director approval.

## Carryover

Because PTO is unlimited, there is nothing to carry over or pay out at termination.
