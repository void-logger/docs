Your first week is about building context. You'll ship something by the end of the week and understand what you're building and why.

## Goals for the week

By end of week one you should have:

- All accounts active and tools installed
- One 1:1 with your manager
- Introductions with your immediate team
- A rough picture of current priorities
- A project to focus on in week two

## Recommended schedule

**Day 1** — Finish the [first day checklist](/onboarding/first-day).

**Day 2-3** — Team intros. Book 20-minute slots with each direct teammate. Ask what they're working on and what slows them down.

**Day 4** — First 1:1 with your manager. Come with questions. A useful framing: what does success look like at 30, 60, and 90 days?

**Day 5** — Reflect. Write down what you learned, what's still unclear, and what you want to tackle first.

## Things worth reading this week

- [Meet the team](/onboarding/meet-the-team) — org chart and team context
- [Company handbook](/policies) — code of conduct, expense policy, remote work expectations
- Your department's section in this KB — Engineering, Product, Design, etc.
