## Teams

### Engineering

Owns product infrastructure, services, and developer tooling. Broken into squads by domain.

### Product

Works with Engineering and Design to define and ship features. The primary contacts for roadmap context and prioritization decisions.

### Design

Handles product design, brand, and content design. Embedded in product squads. For design reviews, ping the relevant squad designer in Slack.

### People & Operations

HR, recruiting, IT, finance, and office operations. Go here for: benefits questions, equipment requests, expense reimbursements, and anything about your employment.

## Finding anyone

Use the employee directory in the HR portal. It includes role, team, location, and Slack handle.

If you're not sure who owns something, ask in `#ask-anything` on Slack.
